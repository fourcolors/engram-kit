# LAB_CHARTER

## Mission

Use NE-1 as the exploratory mother corpus for n=1 memory research. This lab exists to produce grounded observations, candidate architectures, and next-step questions from real slices of the corpus.

## Non-goals

- Do not lock the dataset.
- Do not lock the denominator.
- Do not write paper prose here.
- Do not optimize for final claims before exploration is complete.

## Principles

- Stay lab-first: explore before concluding.
- Keep assumptions explicit and reversible.
- Compare multiple framings before choosing one.
- Favor concrete examples, traces, and probes over abstract summaries.
- Treat coherence, replay, and question quality as the main signals.

## Artifact outputs

- Corpus inventory and sample notes
- Observation log
- Architecture comparison notes
- Open questions and probe list
- Overnight-readiness packet
