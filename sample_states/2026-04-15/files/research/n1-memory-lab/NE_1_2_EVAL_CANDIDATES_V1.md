NE-1.2 window: `2026-02-09 -> 2026-03-08`.

This is the central month of the current `NE-1` arc.
It is not the whole benchmark.
It is the strongest current month for continuity pressure, mixed memory surfaces, and memex-level organization work.

The goal here is not to turn slices into the measured unit.
The goal is to identify windows that can populate reusable continuity templates.
Use the `NE12-W*` IDs below when mapping public eval items back to observer provenance.
